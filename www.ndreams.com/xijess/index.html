What is this?
 
You’ve just begun an adventure. That billboard you saw is a clue – one piece of a jigsaw scattered across the whole of Europe.
 
And even that is just one part of a mystery called Xi. The person at the heart of it all is Jess (that’s her in the video). She was one of the original testers of PlayStation®Home, a 3D virtual world you can access for free from any PlayStation®3.
 
Now her friends need help to find Jess again. What is she running away from? Does it have something to do with the secret project she was working on, codenamed Xi?
 
To join in the adventure, visit Home and look out for the Xi logo in Home Square.
 
Welcome to the mystery.
