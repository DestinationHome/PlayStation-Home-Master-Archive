1. Irem offers the unique "Game space"
Game space 1: Irem square
Irem square is designed and based on the Japanese traditional festival. You can enjoy the fireworks, bon festival dance, sunset, sea and more! You can also get various original items and enjoy mini-games at festival booths.
 




Game space 2: Gathering place for Spelunkers
The game space, or "Gathering place for Spelunkers" is set in a cave atmosphere. Meet with friends and other gamers here and launch into the PS3 game "Minna de SPELUNKER" together.
 




Game space 3: Seaside of Memories NEW!
Seaside of Memories is designed and based on the southern beach resort. You can enjoy the blue ocean, white sand beach, oceanfront cottage, and more!




2. Various Items Offered by Irem
Irem offers a variety of costumes for your "Custom avatar." These include school uniforms, cowboy costumes, Japanese yukatas and many more! Also, unique furnishings can be arranged to decorate at your "Personal apartments."
