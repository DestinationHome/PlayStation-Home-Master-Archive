PS Home Snapshot Gallery

Period: June 24th (Thursday) START

This is a snapshot gallery that contains screenshots of the daily life of PS Home, but we are looking for screenshots at the time of the
"Tokyo Game Show 2010".

Create PS Home locations and avatars to recreate that famous scene or
become that character! !!
We look forward to many posts!

Target period: September 16th-September 21st
Scheduled release date: September 22nd or later

The PS Home management team will select and introduce selected snapshots from "PlayStation®Home (PS Home) Photos" in the gallery!
PS Home Snapshot Gallery is a gallery where you can easily post screenshots taken with PS Home.

Launch the portable item "Camera" and everyone will be a photographer!
I'll take a nice screenshot! You don't have to worry about it.
From casual scenes in your personal space or home square to the decisive moments at an event, feel free to post your favorite photo from the photos stored on your PS3®!

You can post directly from the poster installed in the gallery.
The web browser will start up and My Page will be displayed, so please post the photo as it is.

* Posts are only accepted from PS3®.

  1. The posted snapshots will be selected by the PS Home management team from the photos posted by the Thursday of the week before the posting week.
  2. If you would like to post in the snapshot gallery, please enter "snapshot gallery" in the tag and post.
  3. We will also publish the title, comment, and online ID.

Comments and titles will also be confirmed by the PS Home management team.

Please note that the photo gallery will not be posted if the following content is included.

  1. Real person name, product name
  2. Titles and character names of games and animations that are not handled by PS Home
    * Even if they are expressed using hidden letters, those that can be easily associated with the content are not allowed.
  3. Lyrics of real songs and quotations of publications.

The 2nd floor of the home cafe has been reopened as a gallery space!
In the glass-enclosed gallery space that makes you feel as if you are floating in the sky, there will be a permanent exhibition of the "Snapshot Gallery" where you can enjoy the snapshots posted on PS Home Photo, and only in the gallery space at the time of the event. We will hold a special exhibition that you cannot see.

* The contents of the exhibition will be announced on the PS Home official website at any time.

Whether you're looking forward to dancing or music, or just taking screenshots yourself, be sure to visit the cafe's new gallery space.
