Tokyo Game Show 2010 Commemoration! special event

Period: September 15th (Wednesday) -September 29th (Wednesday)

The game show season has come again this year!
From September 16th (Thursday) to September 19th (Sunday), the major game industry event "Tokyo Game Show 2010" will be held.
* Open to the public will be from Saturday, September 18th to Sunday, September 19th.
PlayStation®Home (PS Home) will also hold an event to commemorate this festival!

Game makers post directly to the official BBS!
Tobira of the game show is open, where you can enjoy live messages from each company to everyone who enjoys PS Home, such as the latest games and event information!
As the name of Tobira suggests, it also links to the "Tobira" screen installed in Home Square during the event. Information that is updated in real time will not be known until the day of what kind of material will pop out.
Please enjoy the information that can only be found here on the official BBS at Home Square!

The movie of the PS3® / PSP® / PS Move exhibition title released at the Tokyo Game Show will be released at the theater.
Check the information on the titles you care about in the theater!
