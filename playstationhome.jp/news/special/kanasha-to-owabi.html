We provide original personal space for free!

From July 6th, we have completely restarted the PlayStation®Network / Qriocty ™ service in Japan. We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience and inconvenience caused by the long wait.

We apologize for any inconvenience caused during the PlayStation®Network / Qriocty ™ service suspension period, and we will provide a "gratitude and apology package" free of charge with a feeling of gratitude for waiting.

The PS Home management team has prepared an original personal space (cubic residence) as a "gratitude and apology package".
Please start a new life in a new personal space.

Beyond the transfer point to your personal space on Home Square, you'll find an innovatively designed building. The intricate combination of cubes gives a mysterious feeling that it is both futuristic and modern.

This "cubic residence" was created in the image of a room in the condominium.

A designer condominium that skillfully combines the design of Japanese and Western houses. We propose to everyone "advanced Japanese" suitable for distribution at PS Home in Japan.

  • The floor plan of the room is not large, but the ceiling is high and the lighting windows are large, so there is a sense of openness.

  • Beside the living room, plants, gravel, and falling water are arranged in the image of a Japanese garden to capture the beauty of nature. It is also fun to put an image of sumi-e in the picture frame and create this as a tokonoma.

  • Bamboo and checkered patterns peeking from the lower floor create the coolness of the balcony. The strong shadows created by the sunlight can be said to be the beauty of Japanese houses.

Maximum number of picture frames installed: 5

Target audience　 : Customers registered on the PlayStation®Network before April 21, 2011
Distribution period : Wednesday, July 6 to Thursday, August 4,
Distribution location : PlayStation®Store

Not available from within PlayStation®Home.
