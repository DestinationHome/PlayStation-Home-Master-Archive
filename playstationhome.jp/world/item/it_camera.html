"camera"

"Camera" is a portable item that allows you to take screenshots within PlayStation®Home.
The captured image is saved on the PlayStation®3 hard disk. Click here

for how to use the "camera"

You can use the zoom function by selecting the first-person view.

On the standard screen, you can shoot the surrounding scenery including yourself.

When shooting, press and hold the "Shoot" button (□) to take a screenshot with the flash fired.

No flash

With flash

This feature is provided in accordance with the PlayStation®Network Terms of Service and the PlayStation®Home Beta Terms of Service.
Observe the following items when using images taken with the camera function.

  1. Do not make any modifications other than changing the size of the image
  2. Do not use for commercial purposes
  3. Immediately discontinue use of images upon request from SCE (or the right holder licensed by SCE)
  4. If a dispute arises regarding the use of images, resolve it at your own risk and expense, and do not cause any damage to SCE (or the right holder licensed by SCE).
