To change wallpaper open the Menu Screen and select "Redecorate".  If the Personal Space you are in supports the use of wallpaper then a "Wallpaper" option will be available. Select "Wallpaper" and then use [LEFT] and [RIGHT] to change the wallpaper.  When you are happy with the new wallpaper press [CROSS] to confirm the current selection or press [CIRCLE] to cancel and stay with what you originally had.  Wallpaper is currently only available in the Harbour Studio Personal Space.
