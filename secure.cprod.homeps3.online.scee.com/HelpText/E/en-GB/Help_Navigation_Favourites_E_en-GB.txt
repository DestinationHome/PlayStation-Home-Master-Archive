Favourites are a way of keeping track of and quickly accessing favourite spaces.  To add a favourite:

<BULLET>Open the Menu Screen and choose "Personal > Add Location to Favourites" to add your current location as a favourite.
<BULLET>Open the Navigator, select any space and press [SQUARE] to add it as a favourite.
