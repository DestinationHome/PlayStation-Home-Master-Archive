Quick chat is a method of communicating with other players using short, predetermined phrases.  Press [L1] to open the Quick chat menu, use [UP] [DOWN] [LEFT] [RIGHT] to navigate the menu and then press [CROSS] or [RIGHT] to have your character say the selected phrase.
