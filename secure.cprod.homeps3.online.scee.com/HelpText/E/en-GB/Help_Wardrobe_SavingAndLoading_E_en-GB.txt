Once you are happy with your character you can save it by choosing the "Load/Save" option in the wardrobe.  To save, choose "Save" and select a slot.  Slots with data in them are indicated by an image of the character, free slots are indicated by an empty icon:

<IMAGE>https://secure.$(env).homeps3.online.scee.com/HelpText/$(RegionCode)/$(HomeLocale)/Placeholder.png

To load a character you have previously saved choose "Load" and then select the slot you wish to load from.  Both the male and female characters saved in that slot will be loaded.
