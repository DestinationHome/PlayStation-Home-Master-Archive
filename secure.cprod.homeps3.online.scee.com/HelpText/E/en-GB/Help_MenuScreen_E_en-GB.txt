Many PlayStation®Home features can be accessed from the Menu Screen: movement to new spaces, character customisation, creating and launching of games and adjustment of settings.  Press [START] to open the Menu Screen at any time then use [UP][DOWN][LEFT][RIGHT] to navigate between the menus and press [CROSS] to select an option.
