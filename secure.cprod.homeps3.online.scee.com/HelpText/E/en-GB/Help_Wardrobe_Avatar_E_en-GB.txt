When in the wardrobe "Character" options include gender, bone structure, facial features, body shape, skin colour, hair style and either facial hair (for male characters) or makeup (for female characters).  Adjust any of these options and then press [CROSS] to keep the changes or [CIRCLE] to cancel before returning to the previous menu.
